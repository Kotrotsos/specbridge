# SpecBridge Financial Projections

SpecBridge targets $5M ARR by Year 3 with a clear path to $120M SOM. As an AI-native company, we expect to outperform traditional SaaS growth benchmarks. Our unit economics support a capital-efficient, high-margin business.

### Target Metrics

| Metric | Target | Industry Benchmark |
|--------|--------|-------------------|

| **LTV** | $2,400 | Varies by ARPU |

| **LTV:CAC** | 48:1 | Best-in-class: 3:1+ |
| **CAC Payback** | 3 months | Median: 20 months |
| **Net Revenue Retention** | 110%+ | Median: 104% |

### Cost Structure (Year 2)

| Category | % of Revenue | Notes |
|----------|--------------|-------|
| **COGS (AI/Infra)** | 15% | Claude API, hosting |
| **Gross Profit** | 85% | High-margin SaaS |
| **R&D** | 35% | Product development |
| **Sales & Marketing** | 30% | Go-to-market |
| **G&A** | 10% | Operations |
| **Operating Margin** | 10% | Path to profitability |

### LTV Calculation

```
Average Customer Lifespan: 4 years (25% annual churn)
Annual ARPU (Y3): $354/seat x 10 seats = $3,540/customer
Gross Margin: 85%

LTV = ARPU x Gross Margin x Lifespan
LTV = $3,540 x 0.85 x 4 = $12,036

Conservative (3-year life): $9,027
```

### CAC Analysis

```
Target CAC: $50-200 per customer

Acquisition Channels:
- Content/SEO: $20 CAC (long-term)
- Product-led growth: $30 CAC
- Paid acquisition: $150-300 CAC
- Sales-assisted: $500+ CAC

Blended CAC Target: $100
```

## Path to $120M SOM

### Market Capture Model

| Year | ARR | % of SOM | Customers |
|------|-----|----------|-----------|
| Year 1 | $160K | 0.13% | 200 |
| Year 2 | $1.74M | 1.45% | 800 |

### Assumptions for Scale

1. **Product-market fit by end of Y1**
   - Clear value proposition validated
   - Strong retention signals (>90% monthly)
   - Word-of-mouth referrals growing

2. **Go-to-market efficiency by Y2**
   - Content flywheel producing leads
   - Sales-assisted motion for enterprise
   - Partner channel developing

3. **Enterprise penetration by Y3**
   - 40% of revenue from enterprise
   - Average deal size increasing
   - Multi-seat deployments standard
